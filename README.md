# Banking-Risk-Analytics-Dashboard
Power BI Dashboard for Banking Risk &amp; Customer Demographics

​Project Overview

​This Power BI project provides a comprehensive 360-degree analysis of a bank's loan portfolio, focusing on Risk Management (NPA) and Customer Demographics. The goal is to identify high-risk areas (Villains) and celebrate top-performing sectors and clients (Heroes).

​📊 Key Dashboard Features

​Executive Summary: A high-level view of Total Disbursed Amount, Outstanding Balance, and Overall NPA %.

​Customer 360° Report Card: A detailed tabular view of 900+ customers featuring search functionality and personalized risk ratings.

​Branch Performance Analysis: A scatter plot analysis comparing Average Credit Score vs. Gross NPA % to identify outliers and top-performing branches.

​Customer Demographics: Deep dive into customer segments based on Age Groups, Occupation, and Income levels using DAX-driven grouping.

​Dynamic Risk Stress Testing: An interactive "Shock Slider" to simulate the impact of rising NPA levels on the bank's health.

​🛠️ Technical Stack

Tool: Power BI Desktop

​Language: DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

​Data Modeling: Star Schema with Dimension and Fact tables.

​Visuals: Scatter Plots, Gauge Charts, Heatmaps, and Custom Icons.
​
​One of the unique features of this dashboard is the "Hero Customer" and "Hero Branch" identification.
​
​📈 Business Impact

​By using this dashboard, bank management can:

​Reduce manual tracking of 900+ customers through automated "Risk Status" indicators.

​Identify specific branches (like Mumbai Main) that require immediate credit recovery attention.

​Design targeted marketing campaigns by understanding which demographic (e.g., Mid-Age Salaried) is the most reliable.
